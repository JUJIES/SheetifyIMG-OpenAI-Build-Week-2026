Sheetify began as an experiment: what if an image model rendered the entire worksheet instead of filling a fixed template? Before Build Week, it was still my private tool. During the week, Codex helped turn it into a beta another teacher can enter through a simple pass. The teacher develops the idea and approves the concept before a draft is rendered. This first pipeline test uses the real Sheetify interface, an isolated demo workspace, and an existing worksheet candidate. The final video will add classroom evidence, measured improvements, and teacher feedback.
